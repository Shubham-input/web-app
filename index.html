Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the training?

Anyone - students, graduates, or professionals from any field. While our modules focus strongly on technology and Computer Science examples, all skills taught are transferable across careers.

Do I need a technical background?

No. Many participants come from non-technical fields. You can benefit from learning how to present your skills, build your profile, and improve communication.

Are sessions live or recorded?

Our sessions combine live online classes and flexible self-paced materials.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes - a certificate of completion for each module and a Career Excellence Certificate for completing all eight modules.

Can I take just one module?

Yes, you can choose any module or package depending on your goals.
